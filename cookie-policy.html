Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies on our website

Good news: Majestic Morocco Trips uses only essential technical cookies. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics that monitor your browsing behaviour.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

Third-Party Services

Our website loads resources from the following third parties, which may set their own cookies:

  • Google Fonts — Used for typography. Google may set a cookie for font delivery optimisation.
  • Font Awesome — Used for icons. Served via CDN with no tracking.
  • WhatsApp — The WhatsApp chat button links to WhatsApp servers when clicked. WhatsApp's own privacy policy applies.

How to Control Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of the website. Here are links to cookie settings for popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

Changes to This Policy

If we introduce new cookies or change how we use them, we will update this page and the date shown at the top.
